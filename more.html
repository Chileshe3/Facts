More Amazing Facts

Honeybees can recognize human faces!

Scientists have discovered that honeybees can be trained to recognize human faces. They have excellent vision and can remember patterns, making them surprisingly good at facial recognition. This ability helps them remember which flowers they've visited and which humans regularly interact with their hive.

A day on Venus is longer than its year!

Venus takes 243 Earth days to complete one rotation on its axis (a day), but only 225 Earth days to orbit the Sun (a year). This makes Venus the only planet in our solar system where a day is longer than its year!

The Great Wall of China is not visible from space!

Contrary to popular belief, the Great Wall of China cannot be seen from space with the naked eye. This myth has been debunked by multiple astronauts. While many human-made structures are visible from low Earth orbit, the Great Wall is actually very difficult to spot.

Your brain generates enough electricity to power an LED light!

The human brain generates approximately 12-25 watts of electricity when awake - enough to power a small LED light bulb. This electricity is produced by the billions of neurons in our brains communicating with each other through electrical impulses.

Bananas are berries, but strawberries aren't!

Botanically speaking, bananas are berries, while strawberries are not! A berry is a fruit produced from a single flower with one ovary. This means bananas qualify as berries, while strawberries, which come from a flower with multiple ovaries, technically don't.

The first computer mouse was made of wood!

The first computer mouse was invented by Douglas Engelbart in the 1960s and was made of wood! The wooden shell housed two metal wheels, and the term "mouse" was coined because the cable attached to it resembled a tail.
